Abstractive Text Summarization using BERT for Feature Extraction and Seq2Seq Model for Summary Generation

The increasing amount of information available in digital formats creates problems in information retention because of a few reasons. Information overload and Time efficiency are to name a few. So my team created a web based text summarizer to shorten long paragraphs without losing important context and information.
